Case 01 · Tax Systems · Multi-municipality Rollout

Municipal tax payment system across 3 local governments

Municipal Revenue Agency & Municipalities — Argentina

🇦🇷 Argentina

Designed and rolled out a unified online tax payment platform across three municipalities — cutting processing time by 45% and increasing revenue collection.

Situation

Three municipalities managed tax collection through disconnected, paper-heavy processes. Citizens had no unified way to check balances or pay online, and administrative teams were duplicating effort across incompatible workflows.

Action

Led end-to-end project management for design and deployment of a centralised citizen-facing tax payment system. Coordinated 30+ stakeholders per organisation — finance, legal, IT, and elected officials — across all three municipalities simultaneously using PMP methodology.

Result

System deployed across 3 municipalities. Processing time reduced by 45%. Revenue collection increased. Platform serving 1,000+ citizens, still active.

Case 02 · Pandemic Response · Citizen App

Government citizen app for COVID-19 vaccination & testing

Government of Catamarca — Argentina, 2020–2021

🇦🇷 Argentina

Led the rapid deployment of a citizen-facing government application enabling vaccination bookings and COVID-19 test result access during the pandemic.

Situation

At the height of COVID-19, there was no digital channel for citizens to book vaccinations, access test results, or receive official health communications. Phone lines were overwhelmed; information was fragmented and unreliable.

Action

Managed end-to-end delivery of a citizen-facing government application under emergency conditions — coordinating health authorities, IT teams, and communications departments. Oversaw requirements, rapid development, security and data compliance, and public rollout at scale.

Result

App launched during the pandemic. Citizens could book vaccinations and access test results directly. Reduced pressure on phone lines and in-person services, providing a trusted government channel at a critical public moment.

Case 03 · CRM · Government Notifications Platform

Custom CRM for official government notification management

Public Sector — Argentina

🇦🇷 Argentina

Designed and implemented a bespoke CRM to manage, track, and dispatch official government notifications — replacing manual processes with an auditable, compliant digital workflow.

Situation

Official government notifications were handled through spreadsheets and email chains — no centralised tracking, no delivery confirmation, no audit trail. This created compliance risks and communication delays.

Action

Designed and led development of a bespoke CRM for government notification workflows. Defined data model, notification lifecycle, user permissions, and audit logging. Coordinated with legal, admin, and IT teams to ensure compliance adoption.

Result

CRM deployed across the department. Eliminated manual tracking errors, strengthened compliance, and created a reliable auditable record for all official communications.
