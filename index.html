Case 01 · Tax Systems · Multi-municipality Rollout

Implementing a municipal tax payment system across 3 local governments

Municipal Revenue Agency & Municipalities — Argentina

🇦🇷 Argentina

Designed and rolled out a unified online tax payment platform across three municipalities — cutting administrative processing time by 45% and significantly increasing revenue collection.

Situation

Three municipalities were each managing local tax collection — property rates, municipal services, and levies — through disconnected, paper-heavy processes. Citizens had no unified way to check balances or pay online, and administrative teams were duplicating effort with incompatible workflows, leading to delays, errors, and lost revenue.

Action

Led end-to-end project management for the design and deployment of a centralised, citizen-facing web system for municipal tax payments. Coordinated 30+ stakeholders per organisation — including finance, legal, IT, and elected officials — across all three municipalities simultaneously. Managed requirements gathering, system development, data migration, staff training, and phased rollout using PMP methodology.

Result

Online tax payment system successfully deployed across 3 municipalities. Administrative processing time reduced by 45%. Revenue collection increased through improved tracking and reduced manual error. System serving 1,000+ citizens and still in active use.

Case 02 · Pandemic Response · Citizen App

Delivering a government citizen app for COVID-19 vaccination and testing

Government of Catamarca — Argentina, 2020–2021

🇦🇷 Argentina

Led the rapid deployment of a citizen-facing government application enabling vaccination appointment booking and COVID-19 test result access — improving critical government-to-citizen communication during the pandemic.

Situation

At the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, there was no digital channel for citizens to book vaccination appointments, access test results, or receive official government health communications. Information was fragmented, phone lines were overwhelmed, and the lack of a reliable platform was creating public uncertainty at a critical moment.

Action

Managed the end-to-end project delivery of a citizen-facing government application under emergency conditions — coordinating health authorities, IT development teams, communications departments, and provincial government stakeholders. Oversaw requirements definition, rapid iterative development, security and data compliance, and public rollout, ensuring the platform could handle high volumes of simultaneous citizen access.

Result

Application successfully launched during the pandemic. Citizens could book vaccination appointments and access COVID-19 test results directly through the platform. Significantly improved government-to-citizen communication, reduced pressure on phone lines and in-person services, and provided a reliable, official digital channel during a public health emergency.

Case 03 · CRM · Government Notifications Platform

Building a custom CRM for official government notification management

Public Sector — Argentina

🇦🇷 Argentina

Designed and implemented a bespoke CRM system to manage, track, and dispatch official government notifications — replacing a fragmented manual process with an auditable, compliant digital workflow.

Situation

Official government notifications — legal, administrative, and operational communications — were being handled through spreadsheets and manual email chains with no centralised tracking, delivery confirmation, or audit trail. This created compliance risks, communication delays, and no reliable record of what had been sent and to whom.

Action

Designed and led the development of a bespoke CRM system built specifically for government notification workflows. Defined the data model, notification lifecycle, user permissions, and audit logging requirements. Coordinated with legal, administrative, and IT teams to ensure the platform met compliance obligations and was adopted across departments.

Result

CRM system deployed and adopted across the department, providing a centralised, auditable platform for all official communications. Eliminated manual tracking errors, strengthened compliance, and created a reliable record system for government notifications.
